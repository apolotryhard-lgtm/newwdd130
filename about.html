Thunder Rapids Rafting Co.

Our mission is to share the thrill of white water rafting with adventure seekers from around the world. Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned rafter, Thunder Rapids is the place where excitement meets nature.

History

Founded in 2005, Thunder Rapids began as a small family business on the Clearwater River. Over the years, we've grown into one of the most trusted rafting companies, known for safety, fun, and unforgettable experiences.
